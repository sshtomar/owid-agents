Who Cares for the Carers?

Nursing and midwifery personnel vs. medical doctors per 10,000 population. Rich nations deploy 100+ nurses per 10,000 people; much of Sub-Saharan Africa operates below 10. The paired bars reveal that in most countries nurses far outnumber doctors, except China where doctors outnumber nurses. The dashed line marks the WHO minimum health-workforce threshold of 44.5 skilled health workers per 10,000.
