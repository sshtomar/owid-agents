Living Longer, Living Healthier

Healthy life expectancy (HALE) at birth measures the average number of years a person can expect to live in full health. This chart tracks each country's earliest and latest available HALE values, revealing where the biggest gains have occurred. Sub-Saharan African nations show the most dramatic improvements, often adding 10+ healthy years in under two decades.
