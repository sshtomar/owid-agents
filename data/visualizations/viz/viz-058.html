The Renewable Divergence

Renewable energy as a share of total final energy consumption (%), 2000 to 2022. Iceland leads at 82% driven by geothermal and hydro. Denmark quadrupled its share via wind power. China's share halved as coal consumption surged, then began a slow recovery. The global average has barely budged: from 15.7% to 17.9% in 22 years.

Source: UN SDG 7.2.1 -- EG_FEC_RNEW (Renewable energy share in total final energy consumption)
