Out of the Smoke

Access to clean fuels and technologies for cooking, 2000-2023 (% of population). Indonesia and China have nearly completed the transition, lifting hundreds of millions out of household air pollution. But in the DRC, Haiti, and Ethiopia, wood and charcoal still dominate -- progress barely registers.

Source: World Bank -- EG.CFT.ACCS.ZS (Access to clean fuels and technologies for cooking)
