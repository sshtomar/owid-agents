Safer Beginnings

Births attended by skilled health staff (% of total) -- earliest vs. latest survey for 15 developing countries. Lines slope upward from an earlier survey (left dot) to the most recent (right dot). Every country shown has made substantial progress, some leaping from single digits to near-universal coverage in two decades.

Source: World Bank -- SH.STA.BRTC.ZS (Births attended by skilled health staff, % of total)
