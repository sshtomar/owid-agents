Living Beyond Their Water Means

Annual freshwater withdrawal as a share of internally available freshwater resources, latest year. Values above 100% mean a country withdraws more freshwater than it renews each year - the gap is bridged by aquifer depletion, desalination, or imports.

A withdrawal ratio above 25% indicates "high water stress" by UN standards. Above 100% is unsustainable without external sources.

Source: World Bank / FAO AQUASTAT - ER.H2O.FWST.ZS
