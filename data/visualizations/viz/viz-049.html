The Global Fattening

Prevalence of obesity (BMI >= 30) among adults, age-standardized, 1990-2022. Pacific island nations lead at 50-70%. Gulf states and Western nations cluster at 25-40%. East Asia remains low but rising. Almost every country on Earth is getting heavier.

Source: WHO Global Health Observatory -- NCD_BMI_30A (Prevalence of obesity among adults, BMI >= 30, age-standardized %)
