The Gender Gap in Life Expectancy

Female and male life expectancy at birth, 2023. Women outlive men almost everywhere — but the gap ranges from barely a year (Bahrain) to nearly a decade (Georgia, Belarus). The widest gaps cluster in post-Soviet states; the narrowest, in parts of South Asia, North Africa, and the Gulf.
