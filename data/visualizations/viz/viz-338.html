A Generation of Forest Loss

Forest area as a share of total land, 1990 vs 2023. Cambodia, Indonesia, and Côte d'Ivoire each lost more than a sixth of their land to deforestation in 33 years. Bhutan, Cuba, Fiji, and China expanded their forests over the same period.

Source: World Bank — Forest area, % of land area (AG.LND.FRST.ZS)
