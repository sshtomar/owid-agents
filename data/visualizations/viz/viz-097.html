The COVID Dip in European Life Expectancy

Life expectancy at birth across 10 EU countries, 1990-2024. The pandemic reversed decades of steady gains: Bulgaria lost nearly 4 years between 2019 and 2021, while western European nations saw smaller but still historic drops. Most countries had recovered to pre-pandemic levels by 2023.

Source: Eurostat -- demo_mlexpec (Life expectancy by age and sex)
