Personal remittances received as % of GDP, 2000--2024. Entire economies now depend on money sent home by workers abroad. Honduras went from 7% to 26% in two decades; El Salvador and Guatemala followed similar paths. For these nations, diaspora earnings are not a supplement -- they are the economy.
