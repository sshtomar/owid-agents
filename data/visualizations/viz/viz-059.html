When the World Economy Stumbles

Annual GDP growth (%) for 10 major economies, 2000 -- 2024. Shaded bands mark the Global Financial Crisis (2008 -- 2009) and the COVID-19 pandemic shock (2020), when output collapsed almost everywhere at once. China and India remained outliers in both episodes, avoiding outright contraction in 2009 while suffering sharp slowdowns in 2020.

Source: World Bank -- NY.GDP.MKTP.KD.ZG (GDP growth, annual %)
