Under Pressure

Prevalence of raised blood pressure (age-standardized, %) comparing early 1990s with the late 2010s. Most countries saw declines, led by wealthy nations with better treatment access. A handful -- mostly in Central Asia and the Pacific -- saw rates climb. Lines colored by direction: blue for decline, red for increase.

Source: WHO Global Health Observatory -- BP_04 (Raised blood pressure, SBP>=140 or DBP>=90, age-standardized)
