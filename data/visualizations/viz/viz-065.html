Capital Flows Across Borders

Foreign direct investment, net inflows as a share of GDP (1970-2024). FDI patterns reveal waves of globalization: China's opening after 1979, the Asian financial crisis of 1997-98, Ireland's corporate tax haven status, and the recent pullback from emerging markets.
