Europe's Innovation Divide: Patent Applications to the EPO per Million Inhabitants

Patent applications filed at the European Patent Office (2004-2024) by country of residence, per million inhabitants. The Nordic countries -- Sweden, Denmark, Finland -- have overtaken the Netherlands and Germany in patent intensity, while Southern and Eastern European countries remain far behind despite gradual gains.
