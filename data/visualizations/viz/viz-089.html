The Tax Divide

Tax revenue as a share of GDP across 25 countries, latest available year (2020-2024). Nordics and European welfare states collect over 20% of GDP in taxes, while many developing nations and resource-dependent economies collect less than 10%. Higher tax capacity generally reflects stronger institutions and broader formal economies.
