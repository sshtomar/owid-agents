Reaching Every Child: Hepatitis B Vaccination Coverage Since 2000

HepB3 immunization coverage among 1-year-olds (%) for 12 countries across different regions, 2000-2024. Bangladesh stands out as a remarkable success story, rising from 0% to near-universal coverage. Conflict and instability in Yemen and Afghanistan have stalled or reversed progress.

Source: WHO Global Health Observatory — WHS4_117 (Hepatitis B HepB3 immunization coverage among 1-year-olds, %)
