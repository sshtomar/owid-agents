Too Young, Too Soon

Adolescent fertility rate (births per 1,000 women ages 15-19), 1960-2023. Bangladesh started above 200 and still records 73. India achieved a 91% reduction. Central African Republic barely budged in 63 years. Angola is the only country where rates rose. Japan and South Korea have virtually eliminated teen births.

Source: World Bank -- SP.ADO.TFRT (Adolescent fertility rate, births per 1,000 women ages 15-19)
