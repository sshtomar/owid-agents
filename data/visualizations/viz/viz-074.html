The Relative Poverty Gap

Proportion of people living below 50% of median income across 50 countries (most recent data, 2019-2024). This measure captures relative poverty -- how far the bottom of the income distribution falls from the middle. Latin American countries dominate the top of the list, while Nordic and Central Asian nations report the lowest rates.

Source: UN SDG Indicators -- 10.2.1 / SI_POV_50MI (Proportion of people living below 50% of median income)
