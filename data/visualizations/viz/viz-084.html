Lighting Up the Countryside

Rural electrification rates from the early 1990s to 2023. Bangladesh and India achieved near-universal rural access in a single generation. Southeast Asian nations followed suit. Much of Sub-Saharan Africa remains below 50%, with Chad and Burundi barely above zero.

Source: World Bank -- EG.ELC.ACCS.RU.ZS (Access to electricity, rural, % of rural population)
