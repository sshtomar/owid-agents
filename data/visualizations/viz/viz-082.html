The Doctor Divide

Medical doctors per 10,000 population, latest available year. Cuba leads the world with 85 doctors per 10,000 people -- more than 200 times the density in Niger. The WHO considers 10 doctors per 10,000 a minimum threshold for basic health coverage; most of Sub-Saharan Africa falls far short.

Source: WHO Global Health Observatory -- HWF_0001 (Medical Doctors per 10,000 Population)
