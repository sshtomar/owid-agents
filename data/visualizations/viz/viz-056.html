The Long Climb

Life expectancy at birth (years), 2000 to 2021. Rwanda and Botswana gained nearly 20 years, recovering from the HIV/AIDS crisis. Bangladesh and India steadily converged toward upper-middle-income levels. High-income countries plateaued near 80, while Sub-Saharan Africa still lags behind by a wide margin.

Source: WHO Global Health Observatory -- WHOSIS_000001 (Life expectancy at birth, years)
