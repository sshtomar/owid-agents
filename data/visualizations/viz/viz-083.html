The Measles Shield

Measles MCV1 vaccination coverage among 1-year-olds (%), 2000-2024. The WHO sets 95% as the herd immunity threshold for measles. While Bangladesh has climbed from 74% to 96%, several European countries are sliding backward -- Montenegro collapsed from 90% to 33%, and Romania fell from 98% to 78%. The US has never crossed the 95% line in WHO data.

Source: WHO Global Health Observatory -- WHS8_110 (Measles MCV1 Immunization Coverage Among 1-Year-Olds, %)
