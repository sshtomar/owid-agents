The Innovation Divide

Research and development expenditure as a share of GDP, 1996-2022. Israel and South Korea invest more than 5% of GDP in R&D, while China quadrupled its spending in two decades. India and Brazil remain below 1.5%, highlighting a persistent gap between ambition and investment in the developing world.

Source: World Bank -- GB.XPD.RSDV.GD.ZS (Research and development expenditure, % of GDP)
