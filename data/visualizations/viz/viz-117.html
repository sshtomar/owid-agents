The Shrinking Share: Freshwater Per Capita, 1961 -- 2022

Renewable internal freshwater resources per person (cubic meters, log scale). Every country shown has lost at least half its per-capita supply as populations grew.

Log scale used because values span five orders of magnitude, from Bahrain (~3 m3) to Iceland (~445,000 m3).

Source: World Bank -- ER.H2O.INTR.PC
