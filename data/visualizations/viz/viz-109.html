The Price of a Longer Life

Health expenditure per capita vs. life expectancy at birth, 85 countries, 2021. Logarithmic x-axis reveals that initial health spending buys dramatic gains, but returns flatten sharply beyond ~$1,000 per person.

Source: World Bank -- Health Expenditure per Capita (SH.XPD.CHEX.PC.CD) and Life Expectancy at Birth (SP.DYN.LE00.IN)
