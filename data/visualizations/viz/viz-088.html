Financial Deepening

Domestic credit to the private sector as a share of GDP reveals vastly different financial trajectories. Hong Kong and Japan exceed 190% of GDP, while Ghana and Kenya remain below 35%. China's rapid credit expansion since 2009 stands out as one of the fastest financial deepenings in history.

Source: World Bank -- FS.AST.PRVT.GD.ZS (Domestic Credit to Private Sector, % of GDP)
